Enrollment Process

For students who are regular, irregular, new, transferees, uniting, cross enrollee, second courser and graduate school.

Step 1: Guard House

  Student: Present valid ID
  Personnel: Check Valid ID and provide Client Satisfaction Survey and Feedback form (ISO)

Step 2: Nurse Office

FOR NEW ENROLLEES ONLY
Look for Ms.Rona Garcia (Admin Building)

  Student: Report to the Nurse Office
  Personnel: Check temperature; Provide Health Clearance Slip

Step 3: Registrar's Office

Look for Mr. Romel De Vera and Miss Dancel Barnachea (GIP Personnel)

  Student: Submit clearance (Last Sem.); Submit lacking requirements; Log In to Documented Information Registry (DIR); Note: for new & transferee students - Clearance is not required
  Personnel: Assist, evaluate and update evaluation sheet; give a copy of updated evaluation sheets; provide list of subjects and units to enroll

Step 4: Program Heads/Course Coordinators

  Student: Present copy of grades issued i.e classcards/CFG/Evaluation Sheets/Compiles IP Grades; copy of subjects and units to enrol to the temporary enrollment forms
  Personnel: Received Clearance (Last Sem.); Received enrollment requirements; Assist and provide pre-enrollment forms: Instruct the students to Log In to the (DIR);

Step 5: Scholarship Office

Look for Dr. Rosemarie Pascua

  Student: Submit duly accomplish pre-enrollment form
  Personnel:Encode subjects, units and bill of payments to the UNIFAST system; Print 3 copies of enrollment form or Certificate of Enrollment and Billing

Step 6: Cashier's Office

Look for Mrs. Caren Joy Ingoy

  Student: Submit Certificate of Enrollment and Billing (CEB);
  Personnel:Collect payments; Sign CEBs

Step 7: Dean's Office

CAS - Dr. Ernest Padiwan (IT Building)
CTE - Dr. Shirley Palma (Gabaldon Building)
CBME - Atty. Carlo Manglioan (Graduate School Building)

  Student: Present (3) copies of enrollment form or CEBs for verification and signature;
  Personnel:Check, verify and sign enrollment Form / CEBs

Step 8: Registrar's Office

Look for Dr. William Pedro

  Student: Present (3) copies of enrollment form or CEBs for verification and signature; Submit ID for validation; Get class cards
  Personnel:Check, verify and sign Enrollment Form / CEBs; Confirm enrollment to the UNIFAST enrollment system; Update enrollment master list

Step 9: Registrar's Staff

  Student: Provide copies of enrollment form or CEBs to the Deans/Program Head and to the Registrar
  Personnel:Check enrolled subjects; Provide class cards;

END
