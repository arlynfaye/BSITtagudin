Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT)

The Bachelor of Science in Information Technology (BSIT) program is a four-year degree that focuses on providing students with the necessary knowledge and skills to design, develop, implement, and maintain various types of information technology systems. In a world that is increasingly driven by technology, this program offers students an opportunity to be at the forefront of the digital revolution.

The BSIT program covers a wide range of topics, including computer programming, network infrastructure, database management, and software development, among others. These courses are designed to provide students with a solid foundation in the technical aspects of information technology, as well as in the practical applications of these technologies.Moreover, the BSIT program also emphasizes the development of soft skills such as problem-solving, critical thinking, communication, and teamwork. These skills are essential in today's workplace, as they enable graduates to work effectively in teams, communicate technical information clearly, and find creative solutions to complex problems.

Objectives of the BSIT Program:

The Information Technology program has adopted the following educational objectives. Three to five years after graduation, the Information Technology alumni shall have:

1

To practice in advance human resource in the field of Information Technology specifically in the development, installation, maintenance, operation and management of information technology;

2

To develop students with critical thinking through research and extension activities;

3

To strengthen values which are desirable in the Information Technology profession and the common good.



Student Portal

A student portal is a web-based platform that provides a central location for students to access various academic and administrative services. It typically serves as a one-stop-shop for students to manage their academic life, including course registration, grade tracking, tuition payments, and communication with faculty and staff. Student portals are designed to make the academic experience more efficient and convenient for students. They offer a user-friendly interface that allows students to easily access and manage their academic information, including course schedules, syllabi, assignments, and grades. The portal can also serve as a platform for communication between students and faculty, allowing for discussion boards and direct messaging
